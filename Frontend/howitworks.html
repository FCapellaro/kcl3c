How It Works

The listing process is very simple and we will guide you through it. Below is a step-by-step guide on the listing process.

Step 1

The first step is simple, simply click the large green "register" button on the home page to create an account with us.

Step 2

In order to start listing your property simply log in via the home page and click "list property", once there you can start to fill in the details and add images for your property.

We suggest having the measurements of your key rooms (especially rooms that are particularly large) before starting the wizard, but you can always save your progress as you go.

When it comes to writing a description we suggest being as brief as possible and listing key features of rooms. Features worth mentioning are fireplaces, bay windows and whether a bedroom is single or double.

For advice on how to take the best pictures for each room click here.

Step 3

Once you have listed your property it will be uploaded to Rightmove, this means that users of Rightmove will be able to view your property details and arrange a viewing.

Step 4

Finally sit back and relax, your property is on the market and potential buyers will begin viewing your home and arranging to come and view it.
