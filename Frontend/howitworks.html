Step By Step Guild To Listing Your Property

Register

Step 1

The first step is simple, simply click on the "register" button in the top right corner of this page and create an account with us.

Create

Step 2

In order to start listing your property simply log in via the home page and click "list property", once there you can start to fill in the details and add images for your property. We would suggest having the measurements and images of your rooms ready before starting the listing process.

Submit

Step 3

Once you have have completed your payment, your property will be uploaded on to Rightmove and Zoopla for the agreed period of time, this will depend on the package you have chosen. Prospective buyers will now be able to view your property on Rightmove and Zoopla.

Track

Step 4

Finally sit back and relax, your property is on the market and potential buyers will begin contacting you to arrange viewings to see your property.
