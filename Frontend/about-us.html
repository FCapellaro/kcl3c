About Us

Home Hunter is an online listing agent that was founded in 2015.
We list on estate agent websites including RightMove and Zoopla without all the hassle and extra fees of an estate agent.
We aims to create the best user experience, with the best outcome, for the best possible cost!
Home Hunter uses a simple system, or 'wizard', to take relevant infromation about the user, and the users' property in order to give the property the best possible listing that will actract viewers and get the most important features of the property across.
Home Hunter also provides useful Advice in Pricing, Media, Viewings and Legal, which includes advice from within the company and external links to important information.
